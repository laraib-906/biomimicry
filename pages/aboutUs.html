ABOUT
THE
BIOMIMICRY
PROJECT

To solve various crisis emerging from the environment, and open the world to biomimicry innovations, this system will use artificial intelligence - powered by a start - up company Spark Beyond to design functional solutions. The solutions to global challenges are all around us, we just fail to realize it.


This project was carried out as part of an academic requirement for degree completion at the Department of Visual Communications at Bezalel Academy of Art and Design, class of 2020.


Lusil Blek is driven by a zeal to explore possibilities of biomimicry and how it can bring a new level of resilience to our ecosystem and economy.
